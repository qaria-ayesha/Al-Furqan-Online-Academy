Welcome to AL-FURQN ONLINE ACADEMY

Our online Quran academy provides flexible and convenient Quran learning opportunities for students worldwide. We specialize in teaching the Quran with proper Tajweed, Basic Qaida for beginners, essential Duas, and foundational Islamic knowledge.

All classes are conducted online via Skype and Zoom. Students or their parents can choose convenient class timings that fit their schedule.

Courses Offered

  • Quran with Tajweed – Recite the Quran with accurate pronunciation and rhythm.
  • Basic Qaida – Learn the fundamentals of Quranic Arabic for beginners.
  • Duas – Memorize important daily supplications.
  • Basic Islamic Knowledge – Understand key concepts in Islamic belief and practice.
